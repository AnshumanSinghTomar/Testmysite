Project 1:

Chikitsak Mobile Application:

Chikitsak is a user-friendly healthcare mobile app designed to simplify appointment booking and pharmacy location.
It enables patients to easily schedule, reschedule, or cancel doctor appointments from their smartphones, reducing
wait times and improving healthcare access. For healthcare providers, Chikitsak streamlines outpatient department (OPD)
management by allowing doctors to view daily appointments and digitally record prescriptions in real-time.

One of the app's standout features is its nearby pharmacy finder, which helps users quickly locate pharmacies close to
their location to get prescribed medicines without hassle. Chikitsak also supports secure digital storage of medical
records and test reports, making health information accessible whenever needed.
With a focus on convenience, efficiency, and data privacy, Chikitsak connects

patients and healthcare providers seamlessly, improving the overall healthcare experience.
Whether you're a patient looking to manage appointments effortlessly or a healthcare
professional aiming to optimize your practice, Chikitsak makes healthcare easier and more accessible.
